FAQ

Common questions

How much does the pilot cost?

The pilot is free for selected waitlist companies. After the pilot, plans start at €49/month — no commitment during the trial.

Do I need internet on site?

Not for site visits and field work. ROVA is local-first — data syncs when you reconnect.

What size companies is ROVA for?

Primarily teams of 3–50 people: painters, electricians, renovation crews, and smaller general contractors.
